What Is Tretinoin Used For?

Acne Control

Tretinoin helps clear existing acne and prevent future breakouts by promoting healthy skin cell turnover, unclogging pores, and reducing inflammation for a clearer, smoother complexion.

Anti-Aging

By stimulating collagen production, tretinoin visibly smooths fine lines and wrinkles, helping to restore youthful skin texture and elasticity for a more radiant, age-defying appearance.

Hyperpigmentation + Skin Tone

Tretinoin targets hyperpigmentation, dark spots, and sun damage, helping to fade discoloration and reveal a more even, glowing complexion that looks healthier and revitalized.
